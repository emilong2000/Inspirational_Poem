    Marriage Tips

    Love his/her Career

    Try to develop love for your partner's profession and encourage him or her

    Divorse is not an option

    Be confident that divorce is impossible. Never consider becoming unmarried as an option. Don't ever think of having another partner. This will guide your action

    Your spouse interest is key

    Consider your spouse interest always before yours. Let his/her happiness be your number one interst. Be happy because he/she is happy.

    Admire your spouse like no other

    Always admire your spouse before he/she leaves home everyday. Be sure he/she is looking good. Make him/her know you are concerned about his outlook.

    Always pay attention

    Always be patient to listen to your spouse even when you have divergent view.

    Obey God

    Obeying and pleasing God should be your family
